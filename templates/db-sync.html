Stored Copies and Fragments

Customer and billing data are mirrored across PostgreSQL and MySQL.

Operational transactions remain readable from the primary PostgreSQL store.

The sync log records cross-database operations for review and reporting.

Operational Notes

Primary writes are validated in PostgreSQL before mirror writes are attempted in MySQL.

Failures in the mirror do not block the main application, but they are visible in operational monitoring.

This supports the heterogeneous distributed database requirement from the brief.
