Digital Water Services

Smart water billing, usage tracking, and customer service in one platform.

WASCO’s digital platform helps customers monitor usage, view bills, make payments, receive notifications, report service issues, and access water and sewerage services online. The system uses Main Service as the operational database and mirrors key transactions to Backup Service to support the distributed database brief.
